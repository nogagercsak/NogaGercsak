Goals for 2025

Jan. 1 2025

"Each atom of that stone, each mineral flake of that night-filled mountain, in itself, forms a world. The struggle itself toward the heights is enough to fill a man's heart. One must imagine Sisyphus happy.”

― Albert Camus

2024 was probably the most insightful year of my life, filled with new experiences. However, going to 2025, I want to find the balance of pushing myself while also embracing being the best version of myself.

In 2025, my goal is to carry forward this lesson. It’s not just about chasing achievements but also about aligning those pursuits with what truly makes me fulfilled. I want to focus on intentionality, ensuring that my goals not only challenge me but also resonate with who I want to become.

As cliché as it may sound, life is fleeting, and the years are passing by quickly. Sometimes, all we can do is put our best foot forward, understanding that our goals demand tremendous effort. Yet, I firmly believe that every rejection, failure, and triumph has led us to this very moment.
